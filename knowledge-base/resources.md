# Learning Resources

The source for the app's **Resources** section: a curated, opinionated starting library for learning
modern go-to-market and GTM engineering. These are the people, publications, and primary sources this
whole tool was built from. Start with one or two, not all of them.

Format (parsed by `app/scripts/build-data.mjs`): `## Category`, `### Name`, then `- what:` and `- url:` lines.

## Newsletters and blogs
### GTM Strategist (Maja Voje)
- what: Strategy fundamentals: motions, ICP and Early Customer Profile, positioning, and the annual State of B2B GTM report. The best single starting point for the basics.
- url: https://gtmstrategist.com/
### Cannonball GTM (Jordan Crawford)
- what: Signal-based and public-data outbound, with real, in-public builds. On-thesis if you care about data-driven GTM and GTM engineering.
- url: https://cannonballgtm.substack.com/
### Growth Unhinged (Kyle Poyar)
- what: Product-led growth, benchmarks, and pricing, backed by real data. Good for PLG and self-serve.
- url: https://www.growthunhinged.com/
### FullFunnel (Andrei Zinkevich)
- what: Account-based and full-funnel B2B, demand generation, and the anti-spray-and-pray view.
- url: https://fullfunnel.io/
### The GTM Newsletter (GTMfund)
- what: Broad GTM strategy, hiring, and curation from an operator fund. Good for staying current.
- url: https://thegtmnewsletter.substack.com/
### GTM Engineer School (Matteo Tittarelli)
- what: Build guides and frameworks specifically for GTM engineers. Closest thing to a curriculum for the role.
- url: https://gtmengineerschool.substack.com/
### Clay blog
- what: The company that coined "GTM engineering". Playbooks, case studies, and the four-layer stack framing.
- url: https://www.clay.com/blog
### Lenny's Newsletter
- what: Product and GTM, with deep interviews (including the Jean Grosser conversation this tool drew on).
- url: https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/

## Primary reports and data
### State of B2B GTM Report (GTM Strategist)
- what: Maja Voje and Kyle Poyar's survey of ~195 B2B companies. The source for the motion-fit and AI-adoption numbers in Benchmarks.
- url: https://knowledge.gtmstrategist.com/p/b2b-gtm-2025-report-trends-insights
### ChartMogul SaaS Conversion Report
- what: Current free-to-paid and trial conversion benchmarks (the replacement for the old OpenView numbers).
- url: https://chartmogul.com/reports/saas-conversion-report/
### Hunter State of Cold Email
- what: Reply and open-rate benchmarks from tens of millions of emails. The source for the outbound numbers.
- url: https://hunter.io/the-state-of-cold-email/
### Google and Yahoo sender guidelines
- what: The actual, settled deliverability rules (SPF, DKIM, DMARC, spam-rate limits). Read the primary source, not a blog summary.
- url: https://support.google.com/a/answer/81126

## Books
